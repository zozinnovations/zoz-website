Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

FocusTime Daily is built by ZOZ Innovations FZCO. This page explains our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

FocusTime Daily is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not operate any servers or collect any personal data.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Focus session history (type, duration, date)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Subscription status (managed by Apple)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

FocusTime Daily offers optional subscriptions (Focus Pro) through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the data collected during purchases.

Notifications

The app may send local notifications (Silent Reminders) as a Pro feature. These are scheduled locally on your device. We do not use push notification services or collect any data through notifications.

Third-Party Services

FocusTime Daily does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only third-party interaction is Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored locally using Apple's frameworks (SwiftData, UserDefaults). We have no access to this data. If you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

FocusTime Daily does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no features directed at children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
